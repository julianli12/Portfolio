About Me



My name is Julian and I'm a Management Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

For as long as I could remember, art had been one of my favourite past times. Over time, I've developed interests for graphics design, illustrations and photography. Out of love for aesthetic design, I became fascinated with building simple, compelling and intuitive user interfaces.

My technical experience as an engineer allowed me to develop my analytical skills and better understand an user's needs. My personal experience in project management helped me develop effective multitasking, organization and time management skills.

Queturn Prototype

Project Description:

Queturn is a social jukebox app where multiple users can contribute to the same music playlist. This app was designed for social gatherings where everyone can vote and contribute to the music playing at the venue.

This video was created to demonstrate the high fidelity prototype, features and functionalities of the app. This prototype was created using Axure.

  • Services: App Design, Prototyping, Icon Design, Interface Design
