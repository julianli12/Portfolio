My Story

I'm a Management Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

For as long as I could remember, Art had been one of my go to past times. Eventually this led to my interests towards graphics design, photography and illustrations.

Out of love for aesthetic design, I became fascinated with building simple, compelling and intuitive user interfaces. I'm a strong believer that in most cases, having an unforgettable user experience is more important than idea in a start up company.

Other Interests

Outside of spending countless hours sitting in front of my computer. I am also a huge basketball enthusiast, volleyball and soccer were the next best thing growing up. In recent years I also picked up snowboarding, golf and Mauy tai.

I'm also a strong believer that travelling is theorapy for the soul, every trip I been on expanded my horizon and rejuvinated my spirit.
